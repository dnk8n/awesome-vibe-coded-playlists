# Detroit Techno Mini-Canon · 1991–1993

**Brief:** two notable Detroit techno tracks per year for 1991–1993 (six total); every track must have
core involvement from a Black Detroit artist (hard constraint). Discogs is the source of truth: Artist and
Track are copied exactly as credited on the linked release page, and every YouTube link comes from the
videos embedded on that same page, validated live against the YouTube API.

**Scope notes:** "Detroit techno" here means records made by Detroit artists in the city's techno lineage,
wherever the label sat (Tresor's Detroit licensings included). Rows sort by the release page's `released`
date; 1992's two rows carry a bare year on Discogs, so they tie-break alphabetically by artist. Artist
identity was verified against published bios and interviews, never inferred from a name.
