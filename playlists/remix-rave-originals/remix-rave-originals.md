# Remix Rave — The Originals (Pre-'00 Sources)

**[▶ Listen — the full playlist on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLPiwGxvXa6To)**

The source half of the pair. Every track here is the pre-2000 record that later producers went back to — the raw material behind **[Remix Rave — House, Garage & Acid Reworks](../remix-rave/remix-rave.md)**. Same fourteen numbers, so original **#8** (Robert Miles' *Children*, 1996) is the seed of that playlist's reworks labelled #8 (Tinlicker, Undercatt and Joe T. Vannelli).

Played in order it's a fast tour of dance music's spine, 1983 → 1999: New Order and Rufus & Chaka Khan open the set; Detroit and Belgium arrive in 1990 (808 State, Age Of Love); the vocal-house and Eurodance years fill the middle (Cajmere & Dajae, N-Trance, Livin' Joy, Gala); and it closes on the class of '99 — Fragma, Alice Deejay, Fatboy Slim — the newest sources the reworks reach back to.

**Method.** Each row links the Discogs **master release** (click "See all versions" there for any pressing), with the Artist and Track copied exactly as credited. The YouTube link is drawn from each release page's own community-attached videos where possible, and every video id was confirmed live before shipping. The Notes name the modern reworks and link straight to their rows in the sibling playlist.

## How the pairing works

- Numbers are shared across the two playlists: each original **#N** is reworked by the entries labelled **#N** in the sibling list. This list is sorted by the original's year; the reworks list is sorted by its own release dates (2017 → 2026), so the two won't scroll in lockstep — follow the number.
- *Toca Me* (#12) is the 1999 instrumental whose riff, joined to Coco's "I Need A Miracle" (1997), became Fragma's *Toca's Miracle* — the thing the modern reworks actually lean on.
- Where a track's most famous dancefloor form is a 1990s remix rather than the first pressing, that's the cut featured (still pre-2000, still the version the rework leans on); the master link exposes every other version.
